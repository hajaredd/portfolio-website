"Un Asiento en la Mesa Marroquí" is an editorial design project

A Seat at the Moroccan Table is an editorial design project that explores the richness of Moroccan cuisine at the intersection of gastronomy, hospitality, and culture. Conceived as a conceptual zine, it aims to transcend the familiar cookbook format, translating a rich collection of experiences—from the aromatic spice markets to shared stories and the rituals that transform meals into celebrations.
